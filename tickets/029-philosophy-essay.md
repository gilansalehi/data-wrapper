  custom elements → most frameworks replace those to get reactivity →
  data-wrapper's bet is connecting them instead → what that costs (be
  honest: the gaps section is part of the argument, not a footnote) → who
  should and shouldn't take the bet.
- Voice rules from the brief §7.1 apply (clear, humble, slightly
  opinionated, no dunking).
